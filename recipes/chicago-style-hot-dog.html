Chicago-Style Hot Dog

Chicago-Style Hot Dog Not to be confused with a REGULAR hot dog. So, just what is on a “ Chicago style ” hot dog? Well, it starts with a steamed poppy seed bun and an all-beef frankfurter. Then it's topped with yellow mustard, bright green relish, fresh chopped onions, juicy red tomato wedges, a kosher-style pickle spear, a couple of spicy sport peppers and finally, a dash of celery salt.

Ingredients to add

NOTE THAT THE Ingredients shouldn't be confused for a regular hot dog
    Ingredients:
  • 1 all-beef hot dog
  • 1 poppy seed hot dog bun
  • 1 tablespoon yellow mustard
  • 4 tomato wedges
  • 2 sport peppers
  • 2 dash celery salt

Steps in cooking

    Steps:
  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the Noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mix
  4. Add layers
  5. Cover with foil and bake
