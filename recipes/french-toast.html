French Toast Recipe This fabulous French toast recipe works with many types of bread â€” white, whole wheat, brioche, cinnamon-raisin, Italian, or French! Delicious served hot with butter and maple syrup.

Don't worry if you do not have the maple syrup, use honey instead!

Ingredients to add

    Ingredients:
  • White Bread
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Vanilla and cinnamon
  • Salt
  • Butter

Steps in cooking

    Steps:
  1. Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt together in a shallow bowl.
  2. Lightly butter a griddle or skillet and heat over medium-high heat.
  3. Dunk bread in the egg mixture, soaking both sides.
  4. Transfer to the hot skillet and cook until golden, 3 to 4 minutes per side.
  5. Serve hot.

Is French Toast Really French?

Nope! French toast (pain perdu in French) isn't actually from France. Legend has it that a man named Joseph French from the U.S. created the sweet breakfast dish in 1724 and named it after himself
